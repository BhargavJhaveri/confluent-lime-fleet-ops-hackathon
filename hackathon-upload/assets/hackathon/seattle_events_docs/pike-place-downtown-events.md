title: Pike Place Market and Downtown Seattle Events
---

Pike Place Market and Downtown Seattle are major tourist and event destinations. Pike Place Market draws 10 million visitors annually, with peak foot traffic between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM on weekends. Downtown hosts the annual Seattle Marathon (November, 10,000+ runners), Seafair Torchlight Parade (July, 300,000+ spectators along 4th Avenue), and numerous conventions at the Washington State Convention Center (capacity 62,000 sq ft exhibition space). Pioneer Square, adjacent to Downtown, hosts First Thursday Art Walk (5,000+ attendees monthly) and Sounders/Mariners game day traffic from T-Mobile Park and Lumen Field. During large Downtown events, scooter usage spikes dramatically as visitors use micromobility to navigate between Pioneer Square, Pike Place, the waterfront, and Capitol Hill. The Downtown core between 1st Avenue and 5th Avenue sees the highest concentration of scooter dropoffs during event hours.
