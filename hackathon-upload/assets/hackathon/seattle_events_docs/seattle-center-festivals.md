title: Seattle Center Festivals and Outdoor Events
---

Seattle Center is a 74-acre campus that hosts numerous large-scale festivals and outdoor events throughout the year. Major events include Bumbershoot (music and arts festival, 30,000+ daily attendees in September), Bite of Seattle (food festival, 20,000+ daily in July), Northwest Folklife Festival (250,000 total over Memorial Day weekend), and the Seattle International Film Festival screenings. The KEXP Music Festival and numerous cultural events draw thousands of visitors. During large festivals, attendees flood into Seattle Center from all directions throughout the day, with peak arrival times varying by event schedule. The area around the Space Needle, KEXP, Museum of Pop Culture (MoPOP), and the International Fountain becomes extremely congested. Many visitors use Lime scooters and bikes from Capitol Hill, Fremont, Ballard, University District, and Downtown to reach the venue, leading to massive concentrations of parked micromobility vehicles within the Seattle Center zone.
