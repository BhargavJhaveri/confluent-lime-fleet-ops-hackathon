title: Climate Pledge Arena Concerts and Live Events
---

Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle Center hosts major concerts and live entertainment events throughout the year. The venue has a concert capacity of up to 18,100 attendees depending on stage configuration. Major touring artists including Taylor Swift, Billie Eilish, Drake, and Kendrick Lamar have all performed at the arena. Concert start times vary but typically begin between 7:00 PM and 8:00 PM, with doors opening 1-2 hours before showtime. The heaviest arrival period for concert-goers is typically between 5:00 PM and 7:30 PM, with a massive wave of arrivals in the 30-60 minutes before doors open. After concerts, which typically end between 10:30 PM and 11:30 PM, the area experiences a flood of 15,000-18,000 people departing simultaneously, creating extreme demand for transportation including rideshares, scooters, and bikes. The Seattle Center area including the Space Needle grounds becomes a bottleneck for pedestrian and micromobility traffic during these events.
