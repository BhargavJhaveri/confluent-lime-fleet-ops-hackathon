title: Seattle Kraken Hockey Game at Climate Pledge Arena
---

The Seattle Kraken play home games at Climate Pledge Arena in the Seattle Center neighborhood. Climate Pledge Arena has a capacity of 17,151 for hockey games. Games typically start at 7:00 PM or 7:30 PM, with doors opening 1.5 hours before puck drop. Most attendees arrive between 5:30 PM and 7:00 PM, creating a massive surge of people entering the Seattle Center area. After the game ends around 9:30 PM to 10:00 PM, a large exodus occurs between 9:45 PM and 10:30 PM. The arena is located at 334 1st Avenue N, Seattle, WA 98109, right next to the Space Needle. During home games, the surrounding streets, restaurants, and bars in Lower Queen Anne and Seattle Center experience extremely high foot traffic. Many fans use scooters and bikes to travel to the arena from nearby neighborhoods like Capitol Hill, South Lake Union, Fremont, and Downtown. The Kraken play approximately 41 home games per season from October through April, with potential playoff games extending into June.
