title: University of Washington Husky Football and Events
---

The University of Washington Huskies play football at Husky Stadium in the University District, with a capacity of 70,083. Home games typically kick off at 12:30 PM, 4:00 PM, or 7:30 PM on Saturdays during fall. Fans begin arriving 2-3 hours before kickoff for tailgating, creating a surge of arrivals in the University District zone. The UW campus also hosts graduation ceremonies (45,000+ attendees in June), major concerts at the stadium, and various academic events. During football Saturdays, the University District experiences a massive influx of scooter and bike dropoffs as fans ride from Capitol Hill, Downtown, and Fremont to the stadium area. After games, the departure surge is concentrated within a 30-45 minute window as fans stream out. Burke-Gilman Trail and University Way are popular scooter routes to and from the stadium.
