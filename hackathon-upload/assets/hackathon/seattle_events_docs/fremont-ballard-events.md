title: Fremont and Ballard Neighborhood Events
---

Fremont and Ballard are vibrant north Seattle neighborhoods with active event calendars. The Fremont Solstice Parade (June, 100,000+ spectators) shuts down major streets and creates enormous scooter demand. Fremont's Sunday Market draws 5,000+ visitors weekly. The Fremont Oktoberfest (September, 15,000+ attendees) fills the neighborhood's brewery district. Ballard hosts the Ballard Seafood Fest (July, 20,000+ daily attendees), the Ballard Farmers Market (15,000+ weekly visitors on Sundays), and numerous brewery tour events centered on the Ballard Brewery District (14th Ave NW). Both neighborhoods have strong weekend brunch cultures that drive late morning scooter surges between 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM. The Burke-Gilman Trail connects Fremont and Ballard and is a popular scooter route, creating concentrations of parked scooters near trail access points, especially at Gas Works Park in Fremont.
