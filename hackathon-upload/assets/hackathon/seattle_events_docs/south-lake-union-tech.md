title: South Lake Union Tech Campus Commute Patterns
---

South Lake Union is Seattle's technology hub, home to Amazon's headquarters campus (over 75,000 employees across multiple blocks), Meta's Seattle office, Google Seattle, and numerous tech startups. The area experiences predictable commute-driven scooter surges: morning arrivals peak between 8:00 AM and 9:30 AM, and evening departures peak between 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM on weekdays. Lunch hour (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM) also shows elevated scooter movement as employees travel to restaurants in Capitol Hill, Fremont, and Downtown. Amazon's campus events (All Hands meetings, re:MARS conference) can bring thousands of additional visitors. The area also hosts the Museum of History & Industry (MOHAI) events, Lake Union park festivals, and the annual South Lake Union Block Party in summer. During evening tech meetups and launch events, scooter dropoffs spike in the Denny Triangle and Westlake areas.
